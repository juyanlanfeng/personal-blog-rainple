ABOUT · 关于

先自我介绍一下！

雨枫是一只温柔又有点小馋的小狼龙。喜欢在雾气腾腾的温泉里发呆，秋天会捧着热乎的烤红薯，冬夜把自己埋进雪和暖汤里。多条蓬松的尾巴是她的骄傲，而生气或开心时，耳朵上的闪电纹会轻轻亮起来。

rainple is a gentle, slightly greedy little wolfdragon. She loves daydreaming in steamy hot springs, holding a warm roasted sweet potato in autumn, and curling into snow and warm water on winter nights. Her fluffy tails are her pride — and when she's happy, the lightning marks on her ears softly glow.
